How to use:

  • Click "Start Listening" or press Ctrl+Enter to begin
  • Speak clearly into your microphone
  • Text will appear in real-time (gray = interim, black = final)
  • Click "Stop Listening" or press Ctrl+Enter again to stop
  • Use Ctrl+Backspace to clear the transcript
Note: This app requires Chrome, Edge, or another Chromium-based browser with microphone access.
